More about us

All around the world, Japanese cuisine is appreciated, respected and most of all, enjoyed. Our core menu is rooted in tradition, therefore every dish we offer has been carefully considered by our head chef and cooked by our experienced kitchen staff members. The seasonal and unique ingredients freshly shipped from Japan, combined with our kitchen’s experience in making Japanese food, we are sure that you will have an unforgettable experience at our restaurant.
